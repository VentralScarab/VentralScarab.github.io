About Us

Concept behind AlgoPlanets

Randomly generated NFTs using Python

AlgoPlanets offers on of the first randomly generated NFT collections on the Algorand Blockchain. The Python scripts can be inspected amd is accesible for everyone on GitHub. We encourage people to build on our code and deploy their own NFT collections. AlgoPLanets will be a pioneer in the Algorand NFT-scene.

Traits

There are over 10,000 possible configurations of AlgoPlanets. Every single one of the AlgoPlanets will be unique, which makes them such special NFTs. After all 512 AlgoPlanets are minted there won't be new ones.

Ranking & Pricing

AlgoPlanets will be ranked in regards to their rarity. Starting with Common, Rare, Super Rare, Ultra Rare and ending with Secret Rare. The exact amount of each category is decided by the script itself. Pricing is fixed throughout the whole collection. Common AlgoPlanets will cost 1 Algo, Rare 3 Algo, Super Rare 5 Algo, Ultra Rare 10 Algo and Secret Rare 50 Algo.

Drops

There will be one drop every week, consisting of 50 AlgoPlanets with giveaways happening inbetween. This will lead to all AlgoPlanets being in circulation after nearly 3 months.
